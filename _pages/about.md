The longest and most profound project I have ever undertaken is one we all share: building a path in pursuit of our dreams. What that path looks like—and what those dreams are—depends entirely on who we are and where we come from.

I was fortunate to grow up surrounded by exceptional role models who helped shape my vision for the future. My family set a standard of ambition and dedication, from cousins who left home to become CEOs of their own companies, to uncles who built global careers, to the two figures I cherish most: my older brother and my father.

Recently, I have had the privilege of building friendships that have profoundly shifted my worldview, adding new mentors to my life. Additionally, my approach to work has been deeply influenced by the biographies of the past century's greatest mathematicians, engineers, and innovators (many of which you will find in the reading section of this site).

Every decision I make, every idea I pursue, and every project I build is a step toward embodying the best traits of these inspirations. I see myself as a mosaic, with each stone representing a lesson learned from them; a unique chemical compound formed from the elements of their legacies.

The person I have become through this journey is who you will find on this site. Welcome to my portfolio. Here, I invite you to explore my professional projects and career achievements, alongside a glimpse into the personal passions—like cycling—that keep me moving forward.
